About Me

I currently am a IT Security Consultant. I do everything from penetration testing to incident response to training and my interests are equally varied. The reason this blog is here is for me to write about things that I am learning about. I have found that I learn things better when I write them down and try to explain them to others, so that's why this blog exists! If you're reading this before I get many posts up I am planning on posting about security topics, programming, and other technical topics related to my personal hobbies.

I have written some other blog posts at The War Room . These are more specifically security oriented, but you should still check them out.
